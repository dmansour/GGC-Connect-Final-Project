Fields of Accounting include cost, financial, managerial, forensic, fund and tax. Graduates can find a career in almost every sector, such as large and small business&nbsp; entities, governmental agencies, health care organizations, software companies, energy companies, retail concerns and entertainment arenas. In-depth study of international business principles ensures graduates are ready for a variety of business&nbsp;&nbsp; careers in todayâ€™s setting of multicultural business relationships. Quality classroom experiences combined with real life exposure and applicationallowstudents to have first-hand experiences before they even graduate.Public and private sector jobs abound and could include Foreign Serviceofficer, diplomat, import/export agent, foreign sales representative, international marketing analyst and international mergers and acquisitions specialist. The MIS concentration focuses on both the business andtechnical aspects of information systems development and also on how decision-support tools and intelligent systems technology can be applied to the effective management of information technology both domestically and globally. Topics include: the global IT environment, project and operations management, IT planning and management, data management, application systems design and development, social networking and cross-cultural issues. View the <a target="_blank" onclick="window.open('http://www.ggc.edu/about-ggc/departments/registrar/docs/program-plans/2013-2014/2013-14-program-plan-business-mis.pdf', '_blank', 'toolbar=no,menubar=no,location=no,scrollbars=yes,resizable=yes'); return false;"  href="http://www.ggc.edu/about-ggc/departments/registrar/docs/program-plans/2013-2014/2013-14-program-plan-business-mis.pdf" >program plan</a> (PDF).</p><h2><a name="marketing"></a>Marketing</h2><p>The B.B.A. with a concentration in Marketing is a great choice for students who want to combine business acumen with creativity.
